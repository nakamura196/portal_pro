This girl is doing, war work for Uncle Sam.Will you rent her a room? She would like to live among us. All she asks is a clean, bright room in Newark or its vicinity.Have you a “spare” room that you can rent her? She is one of thousands of men and women who must have rooms and homes in Newark.The Government demands these rooms for its workers. The people must supply them. It is now the patriotic thing to do, to rent rooms.Register a spare room now, withUnited States Homes Registration Service.City Hall, NewarkJohn J. Berry, Chairman.W. J.
