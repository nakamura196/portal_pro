“It’s for the boy, father!”“It’s for the boy, father!”As a son of William Penn you cannot let that plea go unanswered. From the days of Franklin and Morris, Girad and Cooke, Philadelphia men have set an example of patriotism and liberality in time of financial appeal.Philadelphia calls to you to make her lead the way again. I this Fourth Liberty Loan, Philadelphia must hold no second place; her coffers were never so full; here share of industrial development and national wealth was never greater.So, Philadelphia, “It’s for the boy!” It’s for the honor of Philadelphia’s sons, for the honor of the nation that had its birth in Philadelphia’s Independence Hall!Pledge - not what you can spare, buy what you can raise for the purpose of Liberty Bonds.There is immediate and urgent need for every dollar you can spare. You are only lending, not giving your money. Your Government guarantees the return of your money.The time to act is now!Liberty Loan Committee, Third Federal District, Lincoln Building, Philadelphia（「おとうさん、息子のためですよ！」）
