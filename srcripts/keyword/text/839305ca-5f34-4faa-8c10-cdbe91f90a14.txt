Uncle Sam needs that extra shovelful.Help Uncle Sam to win the war by following these directions:1. Fire small amounts of coal often.2. Keep fuel bed even by putting coal on thin spots. Avoid raking and slicing.3. Keep fuel bed about six inches thick.4. Look out for air leaks in brickwork.5. Increase or decrease steam pressure by opening or closing graft damper in uptake.6. Clean fires when the demand for steam is small, and while cleaning have the draft damper partly closed.United States Fuel Administration（アンクル・サムはそのもうひとすくいの石炭を欲しているアンクル・サムを助け、戦争に勝利しよう合衆国燃料管理局）
