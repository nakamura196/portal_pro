Men of the New National ArmyPrepare Now to Become Non-Commissioned Officers.Arrangements have been made for Men of this city and vicinity selected for our New National Army to receive preliminary military instruction.Competent instructors provided - Drills held on grounds or in halls or armories well adapted to the purpose and conveniently located - No expense entailed in any way.By attending these drills regularly from now until you are called to your Training Camp, you will learn some rudiments of military training such as squad drill, close formation and manual of arms that will give you a considerable lead on your comrades, not so trained in securing the rank of non-commissioned officer.Any information can be obtained by applying at the office of Military Training Camp Association.Phone Spruce 5251Room 117, Commercial Trust Building, 15th and Market Streets, Philadelphia, PA.（新たな国民軍の兵士たちよ備えましょう非任命将校[Non-Commissioned Officers]になるために軍隊訓練基地協[Military Training Camps Association]）
