Why Another Victory Loan?Some of the Needs:1.-To meet the expenses already incurred in in bringing the boys home form Overseas.2.-To meet the payment of soldiers not yet demobilized, including 20,000 sick and wounded in hospitals.3.-To meet the expenses of upkeep of hospitals until the need is over.4.-To pay gratuities to soldiers to tide over the period between discharge and readjustment to civil life.5.-To enable those who desire to settle on the land to do so. (Over 29,000 applications to August 15th).6.-To enable those who desire to take up vocational training to do so.7.-To facilitate the export of our Products by enabling Canada to carry out her national shipbuilding and transportation development.8.-To loan to provincial Housing Commissions the money required for the construction of moderate priced houses.9.-To extend credits to Great Britain to enable her to purchase the products of our farms, forests, fisheries, mines and factories.Such orders are absolutely essential for the continuance of our agricultural and industrial prosperity. The extent of these orders and the amount of employment created will depend on the success of the Victory Loan, 1919.If Canada does not give credit, other countries will, and they will get trade and employment that should be ours. If we fail to finance the Loan, Canada will feel depression.The more Victory Bonds bought-The Bigger the Business done by all.Good Markets- Good Prices will follow the success of the Victory Loan 1919.“Every Dollar Spent in Canada”（なぜさらに勝利公債が必要なのか？1. 兵士達を海外から帰国させるために既に費やした支出を補うため2. 病院にいる20,000人の戦傷・戦病者を含めた、いまだ復員していない兵士達の費用を賄うため3. 必要がなくなるまで病院を維持してゆく費用4. 除隊から社会生活への復帰までの困難な時期を乗り切るための賜金を兵士に支払うため5. 定住の望みを持つ者の希望に応えるため（8月15日までに29,000人以上の申込者）6. 希望者に職業訓練の機会を提供するため7. カナダの造船および輸送手段の整備を遂行することによって、われわれの製品の輸出を促進するため8. 適正な価格の住宅を建設するために必要な資金を地方の住宅整備コミッションに貸し付けるため9. カナダ産の農業、林業、漁業、鉱業、工業製品を購入できるように、英国への貸し付けを増やすため勝利公債 1919年）
